Pros of genetic engineering on our economy

 

Pros of genetic engineering on our economy

While genetic engineering can be bad, it has many beneficial possibilities for our economy, such as, with food becoming more and more plentiful, due largely to genetically modifying it, food becomes cheaper, and therefore more affordable to the average man.

 

2.

Ethical benefits of genetic engineering

 

Ethical benefits of genetic engineering

There are ethical detractors of genetic engineering, yes, but there are also many benefits! For example, if we have the capability to make food more available to the common man, who is to say that it isn't the right thing to do?

 

3.

Health benefits of genetic engineering

 

Health benefits of genetic engineering

While there are many potentially hazardous uses for genetic engineering, there are also many positive uses, such as making stronger antibiotics to combat disease, or making plants more resistant to blight, and so forth.
